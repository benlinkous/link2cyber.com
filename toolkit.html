The Idea

Proof That It Works

Everything on this network — 22 websites, custom layouts, Git workflows, deployment pipelines, CSS design systems, and a 100-project cybersecurity bootcamp — was built by a retired Army Master Sergeant with no formal computer science training.

Not because he's exceptional. Because the tools changed. AI made it possible to move from idea to execution without years of prior experience getting in the way.

The IT Toolkit documents that process honestly — what works, what doesn't, and what becomes possible when you stop waiting to feel qualified and just start building.

The cybersecurity path is at the 100 Projects bootcamp. This covers everything else.
